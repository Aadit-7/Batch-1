3. Animations : @keyframes {}:-

Syntax:- animation: moveBox 2s ease 1s infinite;

- moveBox:-  animation name
- 2s:- duration of the animation
- ease:- timing function
- 1s:- delay before the animation starts
infinite:- to repeat the animation indefinitely
